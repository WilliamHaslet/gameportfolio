Blocko

Blocko is a side scrolling endless runner developed for mobile devices. I handled the full process of creating the game from concept to publication. I implemented game modes of varying difficulty that procedurally place platforms, coins, powerups, and spike hazards for the player to navigate. I built a customization system that allow players to use the coins they pick up to buy skins, hats, trails, and particle effects. I used a third-party hosting site to implement online leaderboards to increase player competition.

I initially published Blocko on Android and iOS, then continued to update the game with more game modes and the addition of trails and particle effects. During development, I play tested the game with the help of my peers and used the feedback to balance the difficulty of each game mode.
